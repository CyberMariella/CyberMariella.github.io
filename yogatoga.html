Sweet Soft Pillow Pose

Just go in your bedroom, lie down in your bed.

For this pose you're going to need three things:
  1. a bed
  2. a pillow
  3. a cup of sugar

Now that you have everything, just relax. Put the pillow on the bed. Now,spread gently sugar all over the pillow.
Lie in the bed, lie your head on the sugar pillow. Breathe deeply.
When breathing out, massage your head. Keep breathing.
